Zurik Truck Academy
Welcome to Zurik Truck Academy. We're excited to guide you on your journey to becoming a confident, skilled, and licensed truck driver. At Zurik Academy, you'll get hands-on training, expert instructions from industry professionals, and the support you need to launch a rewarding career in the ever growing logistics business within the EU.
As a family-owned company with a long standing tradition on logistic efficiency, we serve many well-known large corporations with efficient transport and logistics services throughout Europe. For over a decade, providing credible manpower has been our specialty and we can proudly count on our committed, conscious trainers with the well-known Zurik spirit for their top notch excellence, when it comes to local and international trucking rules and guidance knowledge.
After successfully completing the Truck Driver training at Zurik Academy, our EU and Non-EU graduates can look forward to a job placement within Switzerland or the EU in general. This is because, we have a contractual agreement with an endless list of corporations within the EU when it comes to providing efficient truck drivers.
